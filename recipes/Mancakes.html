Pancakes

The Pancakes the breakfast meal

If you're looking for a breakfast here I give you a choice.

This top-rated recipe is liked by many men so if you want to make something special for your man this is it.

Ingredients:
  • 8 ounces bacon, chopped
  • ½ cup thinly sliced green onions
  • 1 ¼ cups all-purpose flour
  • ¾ cup cornmeal
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • ¾ teaspoon salt
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper
  • 1 pinch cayenne pepper
  • 1 ⅓ cups milk
  • 2 ounces sharp Cheddar cheese, grated
  • 2 large eggs, beaten
  • 2 tablespoons butter, melted
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1 cup warm maple syrup, or to taste
  • ⅛ teaspoon chipotle chile powder, or to taste
  • 1 teaspoon vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon butter
Steps to be followed:
  1. Place bacon in a large skillet and cook over medium-high heat.
  2. Stirring occasionally, until evenly browned, about 10 minutes.
  3. Remove from heat.
  4. Stir green onions into bacon and saute in hot fat until slightly softened, 1 to 2 minutes.
  5. Transfer bacon mixture to a strainer to drain, retaining drippings.
  6. Whisk flour, cornmeal, baking powder, salt, black pepper, and cayenne pepper together in a large bowl.
  7. Add drained bacon mixture, milk, Cheddar cheese, eggs, melted butter, and sugar to flour mixture; whisk until batter is smooth.
  8. Let batter rest for 10 minutes.
  9. Combine maple syrup and chipotle chile powder together in a small bowl; whisk until chile powder is completely dissolved.
  10. Heat 1 teaspoon bacon drippings, 1 teaspoon oil, and 1 teaspoon butter on a griddle over medium-high heat.
  11. Drop batter by 1/4-cupful onto the griddle and cook until bubbles form and the edges are dry, 3 to 4 minutes.
  12. Flip and cook until browned on the other side, 2 to 3 minutes. Repeat with remaining batter.
  13. Transfer pancakes to plate and top with maple syrup.
